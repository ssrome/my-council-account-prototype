Creating a My Council Account

You can use this service to create a personal account which you can then choose to share with council departments.

With an account, you can store and share various personal ID documents.

You will only have to upload documents once and you can choose which council services are able to see a copy of these for any applications you make.

Documents you can upload on this service:

  • Photo IDs (passport, driver's licence)
  • Biometric Residence Permit (BRP)
  • Birth Certificates
  • Adoption Certificates
  • Marriage/Civil Partnership Certificates
  • Proof of address
